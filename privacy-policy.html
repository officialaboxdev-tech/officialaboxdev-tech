Privacy Policy for aMod – Sandbox Game

Last updated: September 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is handled in the aMod – Sandbox Game application.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect or store any personal data directly. However, our app integrates third-party services, including Unity Ads, which may collect certain data automatically.

Data Collected by Unity Ads

Unity Ads may collect the following types of data:

  • Device information (e.g. model, OS, device identifiers)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • In-app activity and interactions
  • Advertising data (e.g. views, clicks, engagement)

This data is used by Unity to serve personalized advertisements and analyze app usage. For full details, please review the Unity privacy policy here: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

2. Use of Data

We do not access, use, or store any personal data from users. All data collection and processing is handled by Unity Ads for the purpose of advertising and analytics.

3. Data Deletion Requests

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, we cannot directly fulfill data deletion requests. If you wish to delete personal data collected by Unity Ads, you must contact Unity directly or follow the procedures outlined in their privacy policy: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. If you believe Unity Ads may have collected data from a child, please refer to Unity’s privacy policy and contact them directly.

5. Security

While we do not store user data, we encourage all third-party partners (such as Unity Ads) to maintain appropriate security practices. Please consult Unity's documentation for more on their data security procedures.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. When we do, the updated policy will be posted on this page with the revised date above.
